# Privacy Policy for RunnerPrime

**Last Updated**: October 29, 2025

## Introduction

RunnerPrime ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RunnerPrime.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect**: GPS coordinates during active runs
- **Why we collect it**: To track your running routes, calculate distance, and map territory
- **When we collect it**: Only when you actively start a run recording
- **How long we keep it**: Stored locally on your device indefinitely; uploaded to our servers only if you sign in
- **Who can access it**: Only you; we never share your exact location coordinates

### Health & Fitness Data
- **What we collect**: Running workout data (distance, duration, routes)
- **Why we collect it**: To provide run tracking and analytics features
- **Integration**: Optional Apple Health integration to sync workout data
- **Control**: You can disable Health integration anytime in iOS Settings

### Account Information (Optional)
If you choose to sign in with Apple:
- **What we collect**: Name, email address (as provided by Apple Sign In)
- **Why we collect it**: To sync your data across devices and enable cloud backup
- **Note**: You can use RunnerPrime without creating an account

### Analytics Data
- **What we collect**: Anonymous usage statistics, crash reports, app performance data
- **Provider**: Firebase Analytics (Google)
- **Purpose**: To improve app performance and user experience
- **Data collected**:
  - Device model and OS version
  - App features used
  - Error and crash logs
  - Session duration and frequency
- **Personal information**: No personally identifiable information is linked to analytics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
1. **Provide core features**: Track your runs, calculate territory, generate route maps
2. **Store your data**: Save run history and territory progress
3. **Sync across devices**: If you sign in, sync data to your account
4. **Improve the app**: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add features
5. **Ensure security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All run data is stored locally on your device in encrypted format
- Territory calculations are performed on-device
- Your data remains private even if you don't create an account

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- If you sign in, data is encrypted and stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
- Servers located in secure data centers
- Data transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication using Apple Sign In
- Regular security audits and updates
- No third-party data sharing

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
- View all your run data in the app
- Export your data (contact support@runnerprime.com)

### Delete Your Data
- Delete individual runs in the app
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Control Data Collection
- Disable location services in iOS Settings (app requires this for core functionality)
- Opt out of analytics (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Analytics ‚Üí Share with App Developers)
- Disable HealthKit integration in iOS Settings

### Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for data collection at any time by:
- Disabling location permissions (will prevent run tracking)
- Deleting your account
- Uninstalling the app
